Help Center

Everything you need to know about using Expenseo

Getting Started

  • Create your account: Click "Signup", enter your email and password.
  • Login / Reset password: Use the login form or click "Forgot Password".

Using Expenseo

  • Adding Expenses: Navigate to “Divide Your Expenses” → Fill in the description and amount.
  • Voice Input: Tap the microphone icon to enable automatic speech-to-text for faster expense entry.
  • Add by Image: Upload a picture of your bill using the camera or file picker. Our OCR system will automatically extract and fill in the expense details.
  • Viewing Reports & Charts: Access “View Expenses” for summaries, tables, and charts.
  • Setting Budgets: Go to your Profile and set category limits.

Account & Profile

  • Edit Profile: Click on your name and select “Edit Profile”.
  • Change email/password: Visit Settings > Account Info.

Troubleshooting

  • I didn’t receive the confirmation email: Check spam or request a new one from login page.
  • My transaction isn’t showing up: Refresh or check your filters/date range.
  • Page loading slowly: Clear cache or try a different browser.

Top FAQs

How do I delete an expense?

Click the trash icon next to the expense item in your list.

Can I export my data?

Yes, we’ll soon offer CSV and PDF export from the “View Expenses” section.

How can I set spending limits?

Head to your Profile > Budget Settings and assign limits by category.

Can I share my account with family?

Right now, one account per user is supported. Family sharing is on our roadmap.

Is my data safe?

Yes, we use secure encryption, daily backups, and never share data with third parties.

How do I use the speech-to-text feature?

Click the microphone icon in the expense input area, then speak your expense. The app will automatically convert it to text and fill in the fields.

How does the “Add by Image” option work?

Simply upload a photo of your bill or receipt. The system uses OCR to extract text (like item names and amounts) and adds them as a new expense entry.
